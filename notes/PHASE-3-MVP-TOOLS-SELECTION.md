## 20 Selected MVP Tools

### Category 1: Legal Research & Case Law (3 tools)

**1. Legal Research Assistant** ⭐ WOW TOOL
- **Description:** Natural language queries to search case law, statutes, regulations
- **Input:** Legal question, jurisdiction, date range
- **Output:** Relevant cases with citations, key holdings, and analysis
- **AI Model:** Claude Sonnet (Pro), Gemini (Free)
- **Complexity:** High
- **Competitor Match:** Lexis+ AI, CoCounsel
- **Why Selected:** Core legal function, high demand

**2. Case Law Summarizer**
- **Description:** Instant summaries of judicial opinions with key holdings
- **Input:** Case citation or full text
- **Output:** 2-3 paragraph summary with facts, issues, holding, reasoning
- **AI Model:** Claude Haiku (Starter+)
- **Complexity:** Medium
- **Competitor Match:** Lexis+ AI, CoCounsel
- **Why Selected:** Time-saving, frequently needed

**3. Legal Issue Spotter**
- **Description:** Analyze fact patterns to identify potential legal issues
- **Input:** Client fact scenario
- **Output:** List of legal issues, relevant laws, recommended actions
- **AI Model:** Claude Sonnet (Pro)
- **Complexity:** High
- **Competitor Match:** Harvey AI
- **Why Selected:** Critical for case intake and strategy

## Prompt Template Strategy

Each tool will have:
1. **System Prompt:** Role definition and capabilities
2. **User Prompt Template:** Variables to inject user input
3. **Output Format:** Structured response format
4. **Quality Checks:** Token limits, formatting rules

Example for Legal Email Drafter:
```
System: You are an expert legal writing assistant...
User: Draft a [tone] email to [recipient] regarding [subject]...
Output: Professional email with greeting, body, closing
```
