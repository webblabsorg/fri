### 8. Risk Mitigation

**Top Risks & Mitigation:**

**Risk 1: Development Takes Longer Than 12 Months**
- **Mitigation:** Launch MVP with core features only (trust accounting, billing, time tracking) at Month 9, add rest iteratively
- **Fallback:** Delay Advanced tier launch, focus on Starter/Professional

**Risk 2: Trust Accounting Compliance Errors**
- **Mitigation:** Hire legal compliance consultant, third-party audit before launch
- **Insurance:** Errors & omissions insurance ($5M coverage)
- **Testing:** 100+ real-world scenarios, beta test with CPAs

**Risk 3: Existing Users Don't Upgrade to PM Features**
- **Mitigation:** Offer discounted upgrade (50% off first year)
- **Bundling:** "AI Tools + Practice Management = Better value"
- **Education:** Webinars showing how PM features save time

**Risk 4: Competitors Add AI Features**
- **Mitigation:** Speed to market (launch before they do)
- **Depth:** 240 tools vs. their 10-20 (hard to catch up)
- **Quality:** Fine-tuned models, better prompts

**Risk 5: AI Costs Spiral**
- **Mitigation:** Set per-user spending caps, throttle if exceeded
- **Optimization:** Cache common responses, use Haiku for simple tasks
- **Monitoring:** Real-time cost dashboard, alerts at 80% of budget
